Ross Ulbricht

Ross Ulbricht is the creator of Silk Road, an online marketplace that operated on the dark web, allowing users to conduct transactions using Bitcoin. The platform was launched in 2011 and became infamous for its anonymous trade of goods and services.

Ulbricht, also known by his pseudonym "Dread Pirate Roberts," envisioned Silk Road as a libertarian experiment in free trade without government interference. In 2013, he was arrested and later sentenced to life in prison.
